Selected Work

Research and Projects

Research Aug 2025 - Dec 2025

Sparse Weight-Decomposed LoRA (SDoRA)

Proposed a parameter-efficient fine-tuning framework that combines weight decomposition with learnable sparse gating. Implemented the SoRA module and trained Qwen2.5-3B models to study sparsity dynamics, pruning patterns, and stability on commonsense reasoning benchmarks.

Computer Vision Sep 2025 - Dec 2025

LLM-Driven Tennis Coaching System

Built an end-to-end pipeline that extracts tennis pose landmarks with MediaPipe and Sport2D, selects informative keyframes, and produces personalized coaching feedback through phase-aware LLM prompting.

Federated Learning Jan 2024 - Oct 2024

Multi-Modality Federated Learning

Reproduced federated learning algorithms including FedAvg and Harmony using PyTorch, then improved the efficiency of multi-modal data processing workflows.

Control Nov 2024 - Present

Water Jet Propulsion Based on Negative Pressure Energy Storage

Constructed control logic in Simulink, explored reinforcement-learning-based optimization, and modeled spacecraft shell structures in SolidWorks.

Robotics Feb 2024 - Oct 2024

Cross Twin Rotor Unmanned Helicopter

Used quaternion representations to implement aircraft control logic and contributed to mechanical assembly for the prototype platform.

Engineering Design May 2024 - Aug 2024

Mars Rover Model
