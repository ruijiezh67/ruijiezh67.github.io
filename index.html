Research and Projects

2025 Research

Sparse Weight-Decomposed LoRA (SDoRA)

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Worked on a parameter-efficient fine-tuning framework combining weight decomposition with learnable sparse gating. I implemented the SoRA module, trained Qwen2.5-3B models, and analyzed sparsity dynamics and layer-wise pruning patterns on BoolQ, PIQA, and ARC-c.

2025 Course Project

LLM-Driven Tennis Coaching System

University of Michigan, Ann Arbor

Built a pipeline for tennis videos using MediaPipe and Sport2D to extract pose landmarks. The project used heuristic keyframe selection and phase-aware prompts to produce natural-language coaching feedback.
